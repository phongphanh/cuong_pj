About Us

We exist to enrich life and the world around us

Since inception, we’ve respected the profound effects that extraordinary built environments can have on people’s lives – it’s why we challenge ourselves to think bigger about the opportunities that we’re faced with every single day.

Xavier Knight offers deep technical expertise in everything we do – our senior leadership team hold a wealth of industry experience, and our business management systems are certified to ISO Quality, Safety and Environmental standards. However, it’s our collective desire to enrich lives and the world around us that differentiates us from the industry.

We’re not afraid to cast aside the status quo in order to design built environments in ways that have never been done before and, after we’ve brought them to life, will never be forgotten.

Our Promise

We bring to life sustainable built environments that elevate human experiences

At Xavier Knight, we enrich lives by consistently finding new ways to add value to all – that’s not just our goal, but our promise.
